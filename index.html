About Marwadi University


Marwadi University has been accredited with an NAAC A+ grade since its founding in 2008. On May 9, 2016, Marwadi University was founded in accordance with the Gujarat Universities Act (Act 9 of 2016).The university is run by the Group of Institutions branch of the Marwadi Education Foundation (MEFGI). 2008 saw the start of MEFGI's operations. The Bombay Public Trust Act of 1950 created it as a core unit of the Marwadi Education Foundation. Marwadi University is promoted by Marwadi Shares and Finance Limited (MSFL) a major stock broking company in India, & Chandarana Intermediaries Brokers Pvt Ltd (CIBPL). a well-known company that trades the Indian stock markets using technical analysis .There are 54 distinct courses available. The NAAC gave it an A+ grade.

What is AAA+ Rating ?

The highest rating given by the agency in its national rating scale for that nation is indicated by the letter "AAA" in the National Ratings. When compared to all other issuers or liabilities in the same nation or monetary union, these ratings are given to those with the lowest expectation of default risk.It denotes the score 80% to 89 percentile. See the chart, Rating Score Rating Description. •AAAAA 95-99 percentile Exceptional. •AAAA+ 90-94 percentile Outstanding.

Why Rajkot?

Rajkot, a city of distinctive urban design and industrial activities, is situated on the banks of the rivers Aji and Nyari. It is a fast-moving metropolis.Its industries of forging and casting are also highly renowned. Rajkot's adjacent industrial districts are national leaders in the production of ceramics, wall clocks, electrical appliances, and a variety of snacks.
